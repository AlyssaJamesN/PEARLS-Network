PEARLS PORTAL

Being an affiliate is more than just joining an organization!

It's about joining a movement dedicated to empowering all girls to achieve their full potential. As an affiliate, you'll have the opportunity to connect with other individuals who share your passions and who are working to make positive change in the world

Our affiliate organizations are located across the country and include programs serving girls in all age groups and stages of development. These affiliates work closely with the national organization to provide services and support to their local communities.

Benefits of being an Affiliate

By becoming an affiliate of the PEARLS Network, you will have access to a variety of resources and support services, including:

  • Access to PEARLS Portal library of resources to support all girls with your organization.
  • Helping with funding through sponsorships and partnerships.
  • Courses and training on program management, leadership development, and community engagement, etc.
  • Mentorship from experienced staff and program leaders.
  • Opportunities to collaborate with other organizations in the PEARLS Network family to share best practices and learn new strategies for supporting all girls.
  • Monthly Community Coffee Hours and other networking events to connect with peers and stay up-to-date on the latest developments in the field.
  • Annual Summits and other events that provide opportunities for professional development and networking.

Requirements to becoming an affiliate

501(c)(3) nonprofit status

A minimum of two years in operation

A commitment to serving GIRLS ages 5-23

A focus on scholarship, leadership,and service

A willingness to partner with the PEARLS Network and adhere to our standards and guidelines
