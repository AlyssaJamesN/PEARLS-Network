Our Mission

The PEARLS Network’s mission is to inspire, empower and nurture the potential in ALL GIRLS, specifically girls from underrepresented communities. Through programs that promote leadership, self-confidence, and academic excellence, we strive to unleash the immense potential of our girls and create a brighter future for all.

We want every girl to know her worth, believe in her own power, and have the opportunity to reach her full potential. We want every girl to feel seen, heard, and valued.

Our Vision

We want to create a community of strong, confident, and capable young women who are prepared to meet the challenges of the 21st century with grace, determination, and success.

We aim to provide our participants with the tools they need to become leaders in their homes, schools, workplaces, and communities, and to inspire them to reach their full potential as individuals and as members of society.

PEARLS Values

The PEARLS Network is built on a foundation of six core values. These values are embodied in the acronym PEARLS, and each value represents a pillar of strength that ALL GIRLS can rely on as they navigate the challenges of life.

  1. Protocol: We believe in the importance of respecting oneself and others, and we teach our participants the skills they need to interact confidently and effectively with people from all walks of life.
  2. Etiquette: We emphasize the importance of respecting others and adhering to the rules and norms of society, both in our interactions with others and in our behavior.
  3. Academics: We are committed to providing opportunities for young women to excel in their academic studies, and we encourage them to pursue excellence not only in their coursework, but also in their extracurricular activities and other interests
  4. Resiliency: We value the ability to adapt, adjust, and overcome challenges, and we work to instill this resilience in our participants as they encounter difficulties throughout their lives.
  5. Leadership: We strive to cultivate the leadership abilities of young women through programs, activities, and mentorship opportunities that enable them to take on positions of responsibility within their communities.
  6. Service: We believe in the importance of giving back, and we work to instill a sense of service and altruism in our participants as they seek ways to make a positive impact on their world.
