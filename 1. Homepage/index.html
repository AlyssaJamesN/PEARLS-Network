Our Mission

The PEARLS Network’s mission is to inspire, empower and nurture the potential in ALL GIRLS, specifically girls from underrepresented communities. Through programs that promote leadership, self-confidence, and academic excellence, we strive to unleash the immense potential of our girls and create a brighter future for all.

Our Vision

We want to create a community of strong, confident, and capable young women who are prepared to meet the challenges of the 21st century with grace, determination, and success.

We aim to provide our participants with the tools they need to become leaders in their homes, schools, workplaces, and communities, and to inspire them to reach their full potential as individuals and as members of society.

About our Affiliates and how you can become one too...

An affiliate of PEARLS Network is a community-based organization that shares our mission and vision and is committed to empowering girls. There are many benefits of becoming an affiliate of PEARLS, some of which includes:

  • Access to Pearls Portal resources and training materials
  • Opportunities to participate in our programs and events
  • Connections to a national network of like-minded organizations
  • Support and guidance from our team of experts

    • If you are interested in becoming an affiliate of PEARLS Network, please contact us to learn more. We look forward to working together to empower the next generation of girls!

    “I’m so grateful to be a part of PEARLS Network. The resources and training materials they provide are top-notch and have helped our organization grow in leaps and bounds. We've been able to participate in their programs and events, which have given our girls the opportunity to learn from some of the best role models out there.” -Willisha Lanksford, founder of Precious Pearls

    FUTURE LEADERS

    In the United States, girls are more likely than boys to experience poverty, violence, and a lack of opportunities. In fact, 1 in 5 girls will be a victim of sexual abuse before she turns 18.

    The PEARL Network is committed to helping girls develop the skills and confidence they need to succeed in today's world. By providing mentorship, leadership training, and community service opportunities, the organization seeks to empower girls and prepare them for success in all aspects of their lives.

    Through its work with affiliates across the country, the PEARLS Network is helping to build a stronger, healthier, and more equitable society for ALL GIRLS.
